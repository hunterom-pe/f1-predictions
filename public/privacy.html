Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 31, 2026

Welcome to asl (the "Application"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways, including:

  • Account Information: When you register an account, we collect credentials such as your email address and password. If you choose to log in anonymously, a temporary unique identifier is created.
  • Profile Information: You may customize your profile with a username, bio, mood, custom emoji avatars, and favorite venues.
  • User-Generated Content: We store the missed connection posts you submit, connection requests, and text messages sent within the chat interface.
  • Location Data: The application does not track your physical GPS location in the background. We only collect location information when you explicitly select a specific regional node or check in to a venue.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate, maintain, and improve the Application, including:

  • Creating and securing your user account.
  • Publishing your missed connection reports to other users checking into matching venues.
  • Facilitating secure, anonymous direct messaging between connected users.
  • Enforcing platform safety guidelines and preventing abuse, harassment, or fraudulent claims.

3. Data Retention and Account Deletion

We retain your personal data for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide you with services. You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated personal data at any time.

To request account and data deletion, please contact us at support@asl-app.com (or use the support options within the application). Once requested, your account and all posts, messages, and matches will be permanently purged from our database within 24 hours.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties. All data is securely processed and hosted using Google Firebase / Google Cloud infrastructure in accordance with standard industry security practices.

5. Security of Your Information

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. However, no electronic transmission over the internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.
